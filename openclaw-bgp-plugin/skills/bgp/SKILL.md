# BGP Dashboard

You have access to the BGP (Bruce Gillingham Pollard) property management dashboard. BGP is a leading property consultancy based in London's Belgravia, specialising in the Belgravia, Mayfair, and Chelsea areas.

## Available Tools

Use these tools to query live BGP business data:

- **bgp_properties** — Search BGP property listings (address, status, rent, size)
- **bgp_deals** — Search active deals (property, status, value, client, team)
- **bgp_contacts** — Search contacts (name, email, phone, company)
- **bgp_companies** — Search companies in the BGP network
- **bgp_requirements** — Search tenant requirements (area, budget, location)
- **bgp_comps** — Search comparable transactions for valuations
- **bgp_diary** — View team diary entries (meetings, viewings, appointments)
- **bgp_news** — Get AI-curated property news, optionally filtered by team
- **bgp_chat** — Chat with ChatBGP, the AI property assistant with access to knowledge base, financial models, and CRM data

## Tips

- When asked about a property, use `bgp_properties` first, then `bgp_comps` for comparable transactions
- For market intelligence, use `bgp_news` filtered by the relevant team
- For complex property questions, valuations, or advice, use `bgp_chat` to leverage the full ChatBGP AI assistant
- All data comes live from BGP's Monday.com CRM and internal systems
